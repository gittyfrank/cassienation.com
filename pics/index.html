Why We Are The Best?

Since 2021, Cassienation Beauty Stop has aimed to perfect the art of nail pampering, manicure and pedicure while building on creativity in nail painting.

We are a team of young, passionate, talented, creative ,and caring individuals dedicated to doing great nails and giving our clients amazing experiences. We keep evolving to keep up with the latest nail trends and also offer our clients the best nailcare services. When you walk into our doors, every moment of your visit should be enjoyable, relaxed and worthwhile.

Our customer service rule is that we treat you the way we would love to be treated.

We maintain unmatched levels of cleanliness and neatness.

We understand that styles and tastes are unique with every individual and our services have been created carefully to meet every taste and style that comes with every unique customer.

Rest assured that whatever your TASTE & STYLE may be, we will NAIL IT!
